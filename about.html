Project

Bomberman Reloaded is a matching game inspired by the popular Nintendo franchise Bomberman. The board starts with a random assortment of colored bombs. Match at least 3 bombs to detonate them. The more bombs you match at once, the more points you'll recieve. Detonate as many bomb-matches as you can and get the highest score before running out of moves.
